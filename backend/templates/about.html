About this tool

The Breast Symptom Checker turns everyday descriptions of breast changes into clear, accessible guidance. It uses a transparent, layered method to help you understand what your symptoms could mean and when it might be worth seeking medical advice. It is not a diagnostic tool.

How it works

Step 1 — Recognises key signs

Looks for important words and phrases using explainable, rule-based logic so results are transparent.

Step 2 — Understands your phrasing

Matches your words to known symptom descriptions, even if you describe things differently.

Step 3 — Helpful in edge cases

If input is unclear, it provides plain-language guidance so you still get a useful next step.

Why it matters

Bridges the gap

Many people delay seeking help because they’re unsure what symptoms mean. We make language simple and clear.

Accessible to everyone

Plain English, supportive tone, and inclusive design help people with different levels of digital and health literacy.

Encourages early action

Gentle, evidence-based prompts guide you toward the right next step if something doesn’t feel normal for you.

Your privacy

We don’t store or share what you type. Your input is used to produce a response for you and then discarded.
